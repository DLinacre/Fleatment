⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING FOR CAT OWNERS: PERMETHRIN TOXICITY

NEVER use dog flea treatments on cats. Many canine treatments contain Permethrin, a synthetic pyrethroid insecticide that is FATAL TO CATS.

  • Cats lack the glucuronidation liver enzyme required to metabolise Permethrin.
  • Exposure causes severe muscle tremors, seizures, hypothermia, and potential death within hours.
  • If you suspect Permethrin exposure, bathe the cat in lukewarm water with mild dish soap immediately and seek emergency veterinary care.
  • Environmental household sprays containing Permethrin (like Indorex) are safe for carpets/bedding ONLY when cats are removed from the room until surfaces are completely dry.
